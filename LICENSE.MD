This repository is intended for learning, discussion, experimentation, collaboration, and continued refinement of the ideas presented within it.

The intent is not to restrict innovation or collaboration, but to preserve the integrity of the broader vision, encourage thoughtful evolution of the concept, and create opportunities for shared learning and collaboration.
